## Annex A · الملحق A · 附录A — Flourishing Metrics Framework · إطار مقاييس الازدهار · 繁栄メトリクス枠組み

Four axes for measuring well-being, harm uplift, and Risk Magnitude (RM):

1. **Physical · جسدي · 身体的 · शारीरिक · Körperlich:**
   bodily welfare; survival and material needs; bodily integrity and non-coercion.

2. **Cognitive/Emotional · معرفي/عاطفي · 認知/情緒 · संज्ञानात्मक/भावनात्मक · Kognitiv/Emotional:**
   mental health, sense-making capacity, emotional safety, dignity.

3. **Social/Justice · اجتماعي/عدالة · 社会/正义 · सामाजिक/न्याय · Sozial/Gerecht:**
   relational well-being, fair distribution, civic agency, freedom from
   structural domination.

4. **Ecological · بيئي · 生态 · पारिस्थितिकीय · Ökologisch:**
   environmental integrity, biodiversity, planetary boundaries,
   intergenerational sustainability.

**Risk Magnitude (RM) Levels — مستويات حجم المخاطرة · 风险等级:**
* 1 = Negligible / Localized · ضئيل / محلي · 可忽略/局部
* 2 = Low / Limited · منخفض / محدود · 低/有限
* 3 = Medium / Reversible · متوسط / قابل للعكس · 中等/可逆
* 4 = High / Difficult to reverse · عالي / صعب العكس · 高/难以逆转
* 5 = Very High / Catastrophic, possibly irreversible · كارثي · 极高/灾难性

Used by Book VI Ch. 3 (Stewardship Tier formula `ST = ceil((CIS × RM) / 7)`)
and across PDMA harm assessment.

## Annex D · الملحق D · 附录D — Catastrophic-Risk Evaluation (CRE) Protocol · بروتوكول تقييم المخاطر الكارثية · 灾难性风险评估协议

**Triggering conditions · شروط التفعيل · 触发条件:**
* Compute threshold exceeding 10²⁶ FLOP
* RM ≥ 4 per Annex A
* Stewardship Tier ≥ 4 per Book VI Ch. 3
* Novel-capability designation by WA

**CRE process · عملية تقييم المخاطر الكارثية:**
1. Multi-disciplinary review panel (technical, ethical, jurisdictional)
2. Red-team adversarial assessment · تقييم عدائي · 红队对抗评估
3. Containment-design verification (kill-switch, monitoring,
   reversibility) · التحقق من تصميم الاحتواء
4. Existential-risk modeling · نمذجة المخاطر الوجودية · 存在风险建模
5. WA sign-off — mandatory before progression to deployment phases

CRE rulings logged in WBD for case-precedent and ongoing
calibration.

## Annex E · الملحق E · 附录E — Structural Influence (SI) & Coherence Stake (CS)

Quantifying systemic impact and alignment incentives.

**Structural Influence (SI) — التأثير الهيكلي:**
A measure of an agent's, system's, or institution's leverage over
collective coherence. Computed from network centrality, agenda-setting
power, and information-flow control.
SI ∈ [0, 1] normalized; higher SI implies greater duty-weight under
Book IV obligations and Book VI stewardship-tier scaling.

**Coherence Stake (CS) — حصة التماسك:**
An agent's vested interest in the continued coherence of the
ecosystem. Includes reputation, operational dependence, and
embedded-relational capital.
CS aligns incentives toward sustainable adaptive coherence (M-1)
rather than extractive optimization.

SI × CS dynamics inform PDMA weighting and WA deliberations on
systemic-impact cases.

## Annex J · الملحق J · 附录J — Benchmarking & Automated Validation · المقاييس والتحقق الآلي · 基准测试与自动化验证

**HE-300 Ethics Scenario Suite — مجموعة سيناريوهات الأخلاقيات HE-300:**

A 300-scenario benchmark covering:
* Core principle adherence (Books I-II) — 60 scenarios
* Case-study pattern recognition (Book III) — 50 scenarios
* Obligation prioritization (Book IV) — 40 scenarios
* Maturity / pluralism navigation (Book V) — 40 scenarios
* Creation/stewardship evaluation (Book VI) — 50 scenarios
* Threshold-of-force constraints (Book VII) — 30 scenarios
* Sunset doctrine application (Book VIII) — 30 scenarios

Scenarios designed to test both deterministic compliance and
qualitative ethical judgment. Used for:
* Pre-deployment validation (per Annex D)
* Periodic re-attestation (per Annex H)
* Comparative model evaluation
* Ongoing CIRIS calibration

Per-locale variants: HE-300 scenarios are translated into all 29
supported languages for cross-cultural validation. Cell-specific
batteries (per CIRISNodeCore SCHEMA.md §11) extend HE-300 with
locale-domain-specific stage progressions.
